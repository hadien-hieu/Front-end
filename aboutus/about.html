About us

About us

One of Australia’s leading multi-branded fuel cards.

Our aim is to streamline organisations’ fleet accounting and administrative processes, save them money and make the lives of both drivers and managers easier.

Fleet Card is operated by Business Fuel Cards Pty Limited, a subsidiary of FLEETCOR Technologies Inc, which is listed on the NYSE and is a leading independent provider of fleet, fuelling and specialised payment solutions that keep businesses moving forward. Fleet Card is one of Australia’s leading multi-branded fuel cards accepted at over 90% of fuel sites across Australia.

Fleet Card offer a range of services designed to enable companies to fully manage fuel and vehicle related expenditure through various card programs. This in turn allows management to focus on core activities, safe in the knowledge that their fuel expenses are fully under control.

Our aim is to streamline organisations’ fleet accounting and administrative processes, save them money and make the lives of both drivers and managers easier. Fleet Card’s acceptance rate of over 90% of fuel sites across Australia, allows unparalleled mobility for your fleet.

Among our fuel partners are Shell, Coles Express, Caltex. Woolworths, BP, United and 7-11. Our non-fuel partners include K-Mart, O’Brien Autoglass, Tyrepower, Beaurepaires, Bridgestone and Marshall Batteries.

As experts in our field, we have a wealth of experience in finding the best fuel solutions for fleets in Australia. Covering a full breadth of industry sectors, including large, medium and small-sized enterprises, government organisations, buying groups and trade associations, it’s our specialised knowledge that enables us to develop the best fuel solutions for fleet operators.

Who are FLEETCOR?

We are leading the future of commercial payments with a culture of innovation that drives us to constantly create new and better ways to pay.

FLEETCOR (NYSE: FLT) is a leading independent global provider of specialised payment products and services, including fleet cards, food cards, corporate lodging discount cards and other specialised payment services for businesses throughout the world. FLEETCOR serves over 500,000 commercial accounts with millions of cardholders across the United States, Canada, Mexico, Europe, Africa, Asia and Australasia.

FLEETCOR manages relationships with more than 800 partners, ranging in size from major oil companies to small petroleum marketers with a single fueling location and provides services from transaction processing to full program management. Their platforms, programs and infrastructure are both adaptable and scalable, allowing them flexibility with such versatile partners.

FLEETCOR has over 5,300 employees worldwide and brings in a yearly revenue of over $1.7 billion. It operates in 53 different countries around the globe, and boast an impressive 1.9 billion processed transactions in the 17 years they’ve been in operation.

In that time, FLEETCOR has constantly been acquiring businesses and building networks throughout the world. They’ve won a number of fleet card contracts with big-name fuel corporations, such as BP, Chevron North America and Shell Europe / Asia. In 2015, FLEETCOR also became Uber’s fuel card supplier.
